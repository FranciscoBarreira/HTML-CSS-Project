100 players walk in. Only one walks out.

If you are new to the Battle Royale genre or find it too confusing, worry not. The task might seem too daunting at first, but following these simple steps will help you conquer The Arena.

Scavenge the Grounds


The Arena is filled with weapons, equipment and cash. Grab it early to gain an advantage over your enemies. Cash will allow you access to different items that can be bought at over a dozen locations spread out across the map.

Choose Your Location


At the beggining of the game, you'll have to choose where you will be dropped in the map. Metropolitan areas will have more equipment to grab, but will likely have more enemies. More remote places will provide you better security, at the cost of valuable items that could be important later on.


Win Battle Tokens


For each enemy you eliminate, you will be granted a Battle Token. Those give you access to the same items you can buy with cash, but do not require you to buy them at specific points in the map.


Only One Walks Out


Survive all the other players to taste glory. Use all your resources and might to avoid being eliminated by other players in the most unforgiving of battlegrounds.
